IBM HR Data Dashboard

Power BI HR Analytics Demographics

Project Purpose

The objective of this dashboard is to analyze recruitment efficiency, employee demographics, and workforce distribution.

Key Insights & Findings

The dashboard provides a complete picture of employee structure, helping identify hiring bottlenecks and workforce trends. It tracks critical metrics such as average monthly income by department, total employees by education field, and average working years to support data-driven HR decisions.
