Adel Emam: A Cinematic Journey (1964-2010)

Tableau Timeline Visualization Cultural Data

Project Purpose

The aim of this project was to visualize the career trajectory of the legendary Egyptian actor Adel Emam ("El Zaeem"), tracking his artistic output and stylistic evolution over five decades (1965-2010).

Key Insights & Findings

The timeline maps out his prolific career, illustrating his transitions through different eras of Egyptian cinema and theatre. By plotting his major works chronologically, the visualization provides a unique perspective on his enduring influence and volume of work across a half-century.
