AAST Parking GIS Analysis

GIS Analysis Spatial Data Urban Planning

Project Overview

This project focuses on developing three multi-level parking buildings and one underground parking lot at the Arab Academy for Science, Technology and Maritime Transport (AAST) to solve the increasing demand for parking.

Dimensions of The Problem

Spatial Dimensions: Limited Space: There’s not much room to expand on the surface. Close to Important Buildings: The parking areas need to be near buildings on campus. Green Spaces and Walkways: We need to consider how the project will affect green areas .

The Objective

The primary aim was to identify the most optimal locations and practical solutions using comprehensive GIS spatial analysis, ensuring efficient traffic flow and maximum space utilization within the campus.
