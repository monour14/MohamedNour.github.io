Diversity & Inclusion HR Dashboard

HR Analytics Diversity & Inclusion Demographics

Project Purpose

The objective of this dashboard is to provide a transparent overview of workforce demographics, focusing heavily on diversity, geographic distribution, and employee retention metrics.

Key Insights & Findings

The visualizations deliver a clear snapshot of a 1.47K employee base across 3 departments. Key findings include:

  • Balanced Gender Representation: The workforce is highly balanced, featuring 45.9% female and 44.3% male employees, alongside a notable 8.4% non-binary representation.
  • Geographic Concentration: A significant majority of the staff is located in California (875), followed by New York (419) and Illinois (176).
  • Young & Retained Talent: The company maintains a relatively young workforce with an average age of 28.99 years, paired with a solid average tenure of 4.56 years.
