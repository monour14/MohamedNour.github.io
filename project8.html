Airbnb Market Analysis: San Diego

Real Estate Analytics Data Visualization Geographic Mapping

Project Purpose

The objective of this dashboard is to analyze the short-term rental market in San Diego, California. By visualizing key metrics such as listing density, property types, and pricing, the project aims to uncover market trends for prospective hosts and real estate investors.

Key Insights & Findings

The dashboard provides a comprehensive snapshot of a highly active market, driven by 5,990 hosts and generating nearly 896,198 total reviews. Key findings include:

  • Market Dominance by Property Type: The market heavily favors unshared accommodations. "Entire home" listings dominate the landscape with 4,573 properties, followed closely by "Entire rental unit" (2,851) and "Entire condo" (1,652).
  • Premium Pricing: The average weekly price stands at a lucrative $4,553, indicating a high-demand, premium coastal market.
  • Geographic Density: The spatial analysis reveals a heavy concentration of listings clustered along the coastline and central urban areas, highlighting the most competitive and sought-after neighborhoods for tourists.
