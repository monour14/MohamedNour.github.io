English Premier League Timing Goals Data

Football Analytics Performance Analysis EPL

Project Purpose

The main objective was to analyze when EPL teams score or concede during matches.

Key Insights & Findings

This visualization shows team strengths/weaknesses across match periods, useful for strategy and performance analysis. By breaking down the 90 minutes into distinct intervals, the data highlights which teams are most vulnerable early on and which have the stamina to score late in the game.
