Tottenham vs. Man City Tactical Report

Football Analytics xG Analysis Passing Networks

Project Purpose

The objective was to deconstruct the tactical battle between two top Premier League sides by analyzing passing networks, ball progression, and shot efficiency.

Key Insights & Tactical Findings

The visualizations highlight a classic clash of styles. The data clearly visualizes Manchester City's dominance in possession and progression, registering 63 progressive passes compared to Tottenham's 38. However, the analysis also emphasizes Tottenham's clinical efficiency in front of goal. Despite having a lower Expected Goals (xG) metric of 1.19 compared to City's 2.08, Tottenham managed to score 2 goals, showcasing their lethal counter-attacking threat.
